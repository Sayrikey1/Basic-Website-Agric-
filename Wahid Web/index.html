VISION


To be in the forefront of agricultural innovations for sustainable agric-economic and industrial expansion in Nigeria.




MISSION


To adopt best practices, research and digitization in all value chains of agriculture and agric-economy to achieve food sufficiency, poverty eradication and economic as well as industrial growth.




Dee Farmers Limited is a diversified, full time family farm that provides wholesome food and sustainably produced products for our community. We are a business that others can learn form, most especially in the area of providing franchise for farming business and empowerment of our youth to be independent and financially secure
We work with a group of experts in the field of agriculture and technology to provide quality service and support to those who have passion for mechanized farming. Hereby assuring nice and quality agricultural farm produce and farm animals, at a low and affordable cost. Which makes food available and accessible to all citizens of the country.
Also, we support the enthusiastic youths, young university graduates and farmers who share our vision of sustainable agriculture, in other to eliminate hunger, unemployment and starvation. We thrive by striving to be as selfsufficent as much as possible, and we believe together we can build a greater, food secure and stable Nigeria.
