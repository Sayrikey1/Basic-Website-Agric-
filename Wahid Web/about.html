At Dee Farmers Limited, through integrated network of farm settlements, we aim to excel in all agriculture value chains, such as cultivation of food and cash crops, rearing of livestocks, fishery, storage, processing, packaging, businesses (local and international) and experimentation and research. We adopt best practices, including IT and digitization in agro economy, industry and development of allied products. We aim for food sufficiency, poverty alleviation, aggressive entrepreneurship, youth empowerment, knowledge sharing, training and franchising for economic growth and dignity of young farmers. Our focus shall cut across all local government in Kwara State and extend to starters in the South West region of Nigeria. Our agic initiative will strive for Nigeria economic diversification by complementing and enhancing agric value chains for rapid industrialisation and agric-economic development.
